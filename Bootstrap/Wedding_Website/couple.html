The Couple

It’s Nick! A true lover of almost all things nerdy, such as Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, many video games, and computer programming, Nick is a true outdoorsman as well. He enjoys hiking, camping, swimming, and almost any outdoor activity you can think of. He has a knack for lawn games, is a talented basketball player, and savors any moment to chop wood. Nick is also a beer connoisseur, although he is partial to a good hoppy IPA beer, and always has an appetite for delicious Mediterranean food.

When Nick is not spending his time with Sam, which he wishes was all the time, he is working for JP Morgan Chase as an Application Developer. Due to this commute into the city every weekday morning and afternoon, Nick has become a bit of a bookworm and has read quite a few books in the past few years. He hopes to one day soon own his own house, with Sam, and have plenty of property to roam around in. Nick is incredibly excited to get married and is actually looking forward to participating in all the planning!

It’s Sam! She’s the beautiful young woman that the nerdy guy above has the privilege of marrying. Sam is something of a creative genius in her writing, cupcake decorating, and various arts and crafts projects. She’s a lover of Disney - World, Land, and the man - but that shouldn’t lead you to think she only vacations to Florida and California. Sam is a tried and true world traveler and has aspirations of bringing Nick to as many foreign locales as possible. Sam has a deep passion for learning and teaching history which not only bodes well for her worldly adventures, but also for her future students (Sam is an up-and-coming middle/high school Social Studies teacher, after all!).

On a personal note, just like Samwise Gamgee, Sam is loyal, loving, and true to the end. She’s the type of woman who will follow Nick down any crazy path he chooses and will always be there to guide him in the right direction. She, too, is incredibly excited for July 16, 2016!
