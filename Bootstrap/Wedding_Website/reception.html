Tappan Hill Mansion

Tappan Hill Mansion is located in the scenic Hudson Valley. Nestled on a hilltop overlooking the Hudson, this stunning stone mansion features amazing views of the Hudson with garden terraces ideal for soaking in the view. We fell in love with how beautiful it and its views are, as well as the history that comes with the mansion.
