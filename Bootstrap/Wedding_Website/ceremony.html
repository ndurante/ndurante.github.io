July 16, 2016
Fordham University Church
What location would be more fitting for us to get married than at Fordham University? It’s where we met! On the very first day of college, Sam walked into Nick’s dorm room, which to his pride and embarrassment, had a black garbage bag as a window shade. Little did we know on that day what we would mean to each other. Over the next four years at Fordham, we would grow to be very good friends and eventually, Nick asked Sam out to dinner one day during our junior year of college. We’ve been dating ever since and now are incredibly excited to be engaged and start our lives together as a married couple.
